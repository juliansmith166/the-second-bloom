How to Use — Step by Step
1
Open this each evening
Set a reminder if it helps — 8pm, 9pm, whenever your evenings tend to get quiet. This takes 2 minutes or less.
2
Find today's day card
Days 1–3 are on Page 1, Days 4–6 on Page 2, Day 7 on Page 3. Scroll down to reach them — or use Print to have a paper copy ready.
3
Click or tap your answers
Every circle, pill, and checkbox is clickable. Tap the urge number that felt most true. Tap any triggers that applied. No right or wrong answers.
4
Type in the "Tonight I feel" line
Just a word or a sentence — whatever is honest. This field is for you, not for anyone else to read.
5
Pick your Win Meter score
At the bottom of each day. Even a 1 — "I noticed" — counts. You don't have to have paused perfectly to log a win.
6
Come back tomorrow night
Your answers auto-save in this browser. Close the tab, reopen it the next evening, and everything will still be here.
What Each Tracker Field Means
1
Urge Level (0–10)
How strong was the pull to text — before you did anything? 0 = barely felt it. 10 = almost impossible to resist.
2
What Triggered It?
What set the urge off — a quiet moment, no reply, a memory? Select all that apply. Patterns become visible fast.
3
Paused 3 Minutes First?
Did you wait at least 3 minutes before deciding whether to text? Yes or no — no judgment either way. Just noticing.
4
What Did I Do Instead?
If you paused, what filled those 3 minutes? Pick anything that happened — even if it didn't feel like a big deal.
5
Did I Text?
"No" is a win. "Yes, intentional" is still a win. "Yes, emotional" is honest data — and it's still data, not failure.
6
Tonight I Feel
One word, a phrase, a full sentence. Write whatever is true. This is your private check-in, not a performance.
The Win Meter — What the Scores Mean
0 I Spiraled Texted without thinking. That's okay — you're still here tracking it.
1 I Noticed You caught the urge before it took over. That awareness is everything.
2 I Paused You stopped and waited. That gap between urge and action? That's growth.
3 I Chose You decided on purpose — text or not. That's self-leadership.
Tips for Getting the Most Out of This
Don't wait for a "bad" dayEven low-urge days are worth tracking. You'll see how your baseline shifts across 7 days.
Printed or digital — both workClick "Print All 3 Pages" from the top bar to get a paper version. Use a pen for the printed copy.
The review on Day 7 mattersDon't skip the Mini-Review on Page 3. It's where you find your real pattern — and your next step.
