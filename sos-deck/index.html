Your 10-Minute Reset (The whole idea in one place)
1. Pause — put the phone face down for 60 seconds.
2. Name what you need — reassurance? belonging? closeness? to feel needed?
3. Do a small reset — use the card in front of you.
4. Choose connection on purpose — from a calmer place, not the spiral.

That's it. Four steps. Ten minutes. Every time.
How to set these up on your phone
To save a card as a wallpaper:
Navigate to the card you want, take a screenshot (side button + volume up on iPhone, or power + volume down on Android), it saves to your camera roll automatically.

To set one as your lock screen (iPhone):
Go to Settings, Wallpaper, Add New Wallpaper, Photos, select the card, set as Lock Screen.

To shuffle through all 10 (iPhone iOS 16+):
Save all 10 cards to your camera roll first. Then go to Settings, Wallpaper, Add New Wallpaper, Photo Shuffle, select just these 10 photos, set the shuffle frequency to "On Tap." Now every time you tap your lock screen, a new card appears.

To set one as your lock screen (Android):
Long press on your home screen, Wallpaper & style, select the saved card image, apply to Lock screen.

The goal: you pick up your phone to text, and the card is right there, catching you before the spiral starts.
